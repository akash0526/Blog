Enterprise Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

1. Minimalist Telemetry & Log Data

We believe in developer privacy. We do not embed invasive third-party ad tracking pixel scripts or sell personal browsing session data. Our platform only records essential technical Edge Web Vitals metrics (such as Largest Contentful Paint benchmarks) to ensure robust server functionality.

2. Supabase GoTrue Secure Authentication

If you are an authorized staff contributor, your authorization credentials (email and password) are securely hashed and managed via industry-standard Supabase JSON Web Token (JWT) cryptographic validation. Strangers can never access administration state.

3. Double Opt-in Newsletter Storage

Readers who subscribe to our weekly dispatches provide their email on an incredibly transparent double opt-in validation model. You can permanently erase your email from our Cloud subscribers database with one single click via your automated footer link.
